Quick Answers

How do I start my first dive in Project Darkwater?
After creating your account and completing the tutorial, head to the Launch Bay from your home dashboard. Select "Begin New Expedition" and choose your preferred difficulty level. Follow the guided dive sequence for your first underwater adventure.
What should I do if I'm experiencing connection issues?
First, check your internet connection and try refreshing the page. If issues persist, clear your browser cache and cookies. For continued problems, please contact our support team with details about your browser and operating system.
How can I recover my account if I forgot my password?
Visit the login page and click "Forgot Password." Enter your registered email address, and we'll send you a secure reset link. Follow the instructions in the email to create a new password and regain access to your account.
Are there multiplayer features in Project Darkwater?
Yes! Project Darkwater features collaborative exploration modes, shared discoveries, and community challenges. You can team up with other divers for joint expeditions and share your findings with the community.
