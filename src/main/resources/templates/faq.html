What is Project Darkwater? +
Project Darkwater is an immersive underwater exploration game that combines stunning visuals, engaging gameplay, and a mysterious narrative. Players explore the depths of the ocean, uncovering ancient secrets and encountering fascinating marine life.

How can I join the beta? +
Beta access is currently limited to select testers. To apply for beta access:

1. Create an account on our website
2. Complete the beta application form
3. Wait for approval (usually 3-5 business days)
4. If selected, you'll receive an email with download instructions

We're gradually expanding beta access, so keep checking back!
Is there multiplayer support? +
Yes! Project Darkwater features both single-player and cooperative multiplayer modes. You can explore the depths alone or team up with up to 4 friends. Multiplayer features include shared discoveries, synchronized exploration, and team-based puzzles.
How do I reset my password? +
To reset your password:

1. Go to the login page
2. Click "Forgot Password?"
3. Enter your registered email address
4. Check your email for reset instructions
5. Follow the link to create a new password

If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact support.
Can I play offline? +
Yes, Project Darkwater can be played offline in single-player mode. However, you'll need an internet connection for initial activation, updates, and multiplayer features. Progress made offline will sync when you reconnect.
Will there be controller support? +
Absolutely! Project Darkwater supports both keyboard/mouse and controller input. We've optimized the game for Xbox and PlayStation controllers, with full button remapping available in the settings menu.
